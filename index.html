About this site

This site is dedicated to displaying projects done during my studies. Updates may be few and far between (depending on workload) but I'll do my best to try and keep things up to date. The "last modified" date is displayed at the bottom of the page (before the footer). If one actively follows my progress they can check the date on each page and see whether its been updated since they last saw it.

My tools

I mainly use C# to script my projects in Unity or Godot, though in Godot I sometimes use GDscript for it's simplicity. I've also learned C++, PHP and a little bit of lua. For modelling I use Blender. I'm also familiar with some photogrammetry software like Alice Vision Meshroom, retopology with Instant Meshes and some predefined asset creation with Daz Studio and Tree It. I've also looked at ArmorPaint and Quixel Mixer for asset painting, though I haven't needed to use them yet. For 2D painting I mainly work with Krita, photomanipulation with GIMP and vector graphics with InkScape. So mainly open source software or otherwise free products.

About the projects here

All the projects on display on this site are projects that I've been involved in to some level (usually as a programmer (to some extent as a modeller (to an even lesser extent as an artist))). Any build of a game that's been published here is supposed to be a working build meaning no crashes or game breaking bugs.
